Our Mission
To inspire and empower students to innovate education.

We have recognized how signficant STEM will continue to be in the world. However, it still does not get the attention that it deserves.

We strive to reorganize STEM education to become more interactive and unique for maximized engagement and understanding.

Why work with us?

Community Impact

When working with InkEdu, you will directly impact communities around the world by teaching them STEM.

Leadership Experience

You will gain unique leadership experience as an officer or instructor for a project.

Service Hours

For your assistance with acheiving our mission, you will recieve volunteer service hour credits.
